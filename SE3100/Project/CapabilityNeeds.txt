Capability Needs:

FUNCTIONAL:
The system shall detect all objects of interest
	The system shall sense objects within a given area
	The system shall determine the initial position of objects within a given area
	The system shall track objects within a given area
The system shall assess relevant data
	The system shall continually evaluate all threat vectors (direction and velocity of tracked threats)
	The system shall continually classify object based on their perceived threat
The system shall communicate with appropriate personnel and systems
	The system shall warn appropriate personnel of pending threat
	The system shall record all activities for future evaluation
	The system shall feed data as necessary to other systems
The system shall defend personnel, facilities, and equipment
	The system shall engage threat
	The system shall neutralize the threat

NON-FUNCTIONAL:
Transportability of system
Reliability of system
Supportability of system
Maintainability of system
