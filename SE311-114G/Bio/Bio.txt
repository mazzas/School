Steven Mazza is a Computer Engineer and the CERDEC Command, Power, and Integration (CP&I) Directorate Mission Command Capabilities Division Handheld Technologies Branch Project Lead for the Mission Command Technology Enabled Capability Demonstration (TECD).  Projects in the Mission Command TECD under his direction include a language translation effort, work with unmanned systems, development for soldier power management, and ongoing work for Virtual Pointer (an augmented reality gun scope) and TacMark (the Army's tactical marketplace).  Mr. Mazza has domain expertise in both enterprise systems and commercial hand held development and has been recently published in Army Times and the C4ISR Journal.
